Atlantic City

Atlantic City, founded in 1854, is an urban city located on the southern side of New Jersey. Its population as of 2020 is 38,497. The average income in Atlantic City is $29,773, compared to the state at $89,703 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.). Atlantic City is one of the most popular cities in the state for tourists to visit, known for its casinos, beaches, and restaurants.

Popular things to do in Atlantic City

  1. Have fun at Atlantic City Boardwalk.
  2. Stay at Borgata Hotel Casino and Spa.
  3. Find Amusement at Steel Pier Amusement Park.

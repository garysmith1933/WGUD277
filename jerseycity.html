Jersey City

Jersey City, founded in 1820, is an urban city located on the northeastern side of New Jersey. Its population as of 2020 is 292,449. The average income in Atlantic City is $81,390, compared to the state's average income at $89,703 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.). Jersey City is one of the most popular cities in the state to live in, known for its parks, incredible views of the Manhattan Skyline, and nightlife.

Popular things to do in Jersey City

  1. Explore Liberty State Park.
  2. Educate yourself at Liberty Science Center.
  3. Enjoy the Sea The City Tour.

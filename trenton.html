Trenton

Trenton, the capital of New Jersey, founded in 1679, is an suburban city located in the central part of New Jersey (Yes, central jersey exits). Its population as of 2020 is 90,871. The average income in Trenton is $39,718 (U.S. Census Bureau, n.d.) Trenton is considered to be a historical city. Known for The Battle of Trenton and being one of the worlds leading manufacturers in the 1920s.

Popular things to do in Trenton

  2. Visit the New Jersey State Museum.
